Who We Are

More Than a Store.
A Community.

What are you ready for? At Tennessee Readiness, that's not just a question — it's a challenge we help you answer every day. Your Smoky Mountain source for camping, hiking, and emergency preparedness, we're nestled in the heart of East Tennessee with one mission: making sure you're never caught unprepared.

From your first overnight trail to building a family emergency plan, we offer expert-led seminars, hands-on training, and gear you can trust. Come in with questions. Leave ready for anything.

The Porch

Pull up a rocker. No agenda, no schedule — just mountain air, good music, checkers, and the best neighbors in Cosby, Tennessee.

🪕
Local Musicians

Local musicians drop by and fill the porch with everything from bluegrass to folk. You never quite know who'll be picking when you arrive — and that's the beauty of it.

Rocking Chairs

Slow down and take in the Smoky Mountain views from a proper rocking chair. The kind of seat where an hour disappears without you noticing.

🏁
Checkers

Challenge a stranger to a game of checkers. Regulars will warn you — some of the local characters have been playing since before you were born.

🤠
Local Characters

The porch regulars are half the reason people keep coming back. Storytellers, jokers, and genuine mountain folk who make every visit feel like coming home.

Smoky Mountain
Gift Shop

Take a little piece of the mountains home. Our gift shop is stocked with handcrafted treasures from local artisans, Smoky Mountain keepsakes, and a whole lot of Sasquatch.

🎻
Local Artisan Crafts

Handcrafted goods made right here in East Tennessee — including handmade dulcimers, borer bee traps, woodwork, and more. Every piece tells a story.

🦶
Sasquatch Central

He's out there somewhere — and we've got the gear to prove it. T-shirts, plush, stickers, signs, and all manner of Bigfoot swag for the true believers.

🍬
Candy & Treats

Old-fashioned candy, mountain honey, jams, and other sweet treats — the kind you'd expect to find at a proper Smoky Mountain general store.

🏔️
Smoky Mountain Keepsakes

Magnets, mugs, ornaments, and all the classic mountain trinkets — perfect for taking home a memory or finding the right gift for someone who loves these hills.
